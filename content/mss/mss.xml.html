Worcester

  • Manuscript Sigil: Wor
  • Manuscript Short Name: Worcester
  • Manuscript Filename: worcester.xml
  • Manuscript URL: /ms/worcester
  • Range of Manuscript Edited: Dominica 1 Adventus until Feria 2 Hebdomada 3 Quadragesimae
  • Manuscript Details:

    The Worcester Antiphoner. Worcester Cathedral Library Ms F. 160. A composite manuscript of liturgical books from the Benedictine Cathedral Priory of the Blessed Virgin Mary, Worcester probably compiled in its present form in the fourteenth/fifteenth century. The oldest contents, dating from the first part of the thirteenth century, are the Office Antiphoner, Calendar, Psalter, Hymnal and Collectar. The Processional and Gradual date from slightly later. Various other sections were added in the fourteenth and fifteenth centuries. The Office Antiphoner, Processional and Hymnal sections were published in a photographic reproduction in Antiphonaire Monastique, XIIIe siecle: Codex F. 160 de la Bibliotheque de la Cathedrale de Worcester (Paleographie Musicale volume 12) Solesmes 1922, reprinted Berne 1971.
